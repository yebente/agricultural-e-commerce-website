5 Tips for Sustainable Home Gardening

April 15, 2025

Home gardening can be rewarding when done sustainably. Start by using compost to enrich your soil naturally, practice crop rotation to prevent soil depletion, collect rainwater for irrigation, choose indigenous plants suited to your climate, and avoid chemical pesticides by using organic alternatives like neem oil. These simple changes make your garden healthier and more eco-friendly.

How We Keep Our Bees Happy

May 4, 2025

Bees are vital to our farm's ecosystem. We practice natural beekeeping by avoiding chemical treatments and allowing bees to build their own comb. We plant nectar-rich flowers nearby to provide ample foraging and ensure hives are well-ventilated and shaded. By creating a calm and healthy environment, we harvest pure honey and contribute to pollination across our farm.

New Planting Season Begins

May 11, 2025

This season, we're planting maize, cowpeas, green grams and kales. Before planting, we test soil health and apply organic manure to improve fertility. We also consider rainfall forecasts and space crops properly to maximize yields. Our team is optimistic about achieving a productive harvest thanks to careful planning and teamwork from everyone on the farm.
