About us

Good Times Farm is a youth-led agribusiness empowering communities in Kaloleni and beyond through a unique blend of traditional agriculture and modern innovation. Our roots are deeply embedded in sustainable farming, but our vision is planted firmly in the future.

From humble beginnings raising poultry and growing cassava, we are now transitioning into a tech-driven farming model. By integrating mobile data collection tools, IoT sensors for crop monitoring, and digital marketing platforms, we're reshaping how smallholders access knowledge, track productivity, and reach markets.

Our goal is to inspire young people to see agriculture not just as a way of life, but as a viable, smart career. Through training, mentorship, and digital integration, we bridge the gap between manual labor and intelligent systems.

Good Times Company empowers youth and communities through innovative, sustainable agriculture. We train young farmers in poultry, boost honey production with modern beekeeping, and improve dairy yields using zero-grazing systems with crossbred cows. Our crop initiatives promote drought-tolerant cassava, improved cowpea for food security, and hybrid watermelon for youth income. These projects strengthen value chains and uplift local livelihoods.

Our Core Projects

Fruit

Watermelon Farming

We cultivate hybrid watermelon varieties like Sukari F1, ensuring high yields and quality. Our project focuses on proper irrigation, pest control, and market linkage to enhance income for youth groups.

Poultry Rearing

We run a hands-on poultry training project that focuses on best practices in broiler and Kienyeji chicken farming. Through demo farms and mentorship, we empower youth and women with skills in housing design, feed formulation, and vaccination routines.

Bees

Apiculture (Beekeeping)

We maintain an expanding apiary using Langstroth and Kenya Top Bar hives, promoting both productivity and sustainability. Our bees produce high-quality organic honey, and their pollination efforts improve yields across nearby farms.

Livestock

Dairy Farming

We rear high-yield crossbreeds including Friesian, Ayrshire, and Jersey cows under a zero-grazing system. Our focus is on proper nutrition, regular deworming, and animal hygiene to boost milk production and quality.

Livestock

Beef Cattle Rearing

We raise indigenous breeds like Zebu and crossbreeds such as Sahiwal and Boran, utilizing pasture rotation, mineral supplementation, and fattening techniques. Our beef program ensures market-ready livestock.

Crops

Cassava Farming

We cultivate improved cassava varieties including KME 1, Tajirika, and Mkuranga under intercropping systems. These varieties offer high yields, early maturity, and resistance to mosaic disease.

Crops

Cowpea Farming

We grow cowpea varieties such as M66 and KVU 27-1, chosen for their dual-purpose use (leaves and grains) and resilience in semi-arid climates. Integrated pest management and organic inputs maintain crop health.

Our Future Vision: Smart Farming

We are building a digital platform where farmers can:

  • Receive weather alerts and planting advice based on real-time data
  • Access training videos and virtual mentorships
  • Use digital records to manage livestock and crop cycles efficiently
  • Sell produce directly through our e-commerce shop
Our journey from basic tools to precision farming is fueled by the belief that technology can uplift rural livelihoods and create decent jobs for youth in agriculture.
